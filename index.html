Seabreeze Couriers is one of the leading courier distribution companies in GCC and India covering both domestic and international services. Incorporated in the year 1998, Seabreeze is in its 13th year of operations in India and UAE. Sea breeze Couriers takes the credit of pioneering the franchisee concept for the courier industry between India and UAE, and today has the largest courier network serving retail and SME customers in India and GCC. It has a large delivery network covering all the major locations in India especially Kerala where they assure a express delivery time of maximum four days in delivering your gift to your beloved ones.

The team at SeaBreeeze Couriers comprises of experienced people in the cargo and courier industry and has wide experience in working across GCC and India.Its efficient network spans over different destinations supported by its own offices in U.A.E [Bur Dubai,Deira,Al Qouz, Sharjah, Ajman, Abu Dhabi,Musaffah] K.S.A[Jeddah, Damman] and as well as associate offices in all the important Indian destinations.
